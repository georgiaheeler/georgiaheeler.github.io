About

My Story

Hi there, I’m Georgia. UI/UX designer, psychology graduate and avid knitter, I ventured into the world of UX design as a way of combining my analytical and creative skills with my people-focused way of thinking.

As humans, we like to think we are rational beings. However, observations from history, research and everyday life continue to demonstrate we do not always think or act in ways we would expect to. This reaffirms the importance of acquiring first-hand insight from users to inform our design decisions – a practice I stand by during all my design projects.

I’m looking to work for a London-based organisation (or remote opportunity) that values transparency and employees’ professional and personal development. I am keen to work in a fast-paced and collaborative environment, where teamwork, feedback and iteration forms the backbone of the impactful delivery of user-centred products and services.
