My Story

Hi there, I’m Georgia. As a Content Designer with a Psychology BSc, I ventured into the world of Product Design as a way of combining my creative and analytical skills with my people-focused way of thinking.

As humans, we like to think we are rational beings. However, observations from history, research, and everyday life continue to demonstrate we do not always think or act in ways we would expect. This reaffirms the importance of acquiring first-hand insight from users to inform our design decisions – a practice I stand by in all my design projects.

I am looking to work for a socially driven organisation where teamwork, feedback and iteration forms the backbone of the impactful delivery of user-centred products and services.

When I’m not busy pushing pixels you can find me knitting for my Etsy shop, browsing second-hand marketplaces, trialing veggie recipes or busting out moves on the dance-floor.
